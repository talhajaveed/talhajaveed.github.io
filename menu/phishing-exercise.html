Phishing Exercise

Phishing is a fraudulent attempt usually made through email communications that aim to steal your personal information such as credit card number, social security number, account number or password. The best way to protect yourself from phishing is to learn how to recognize a phish. Training employees on how to recognize these malicious emails is a must for enterprises who wish to prevent sensitive data loss.

Phishing emails will almost always tell you to click a link that takes you to a site where your personal information is requested. Most often, these emails are written under such a form as to appear to be from a company the recipient knows or does business with.

Phishing Exercise Process

Our team will perform a simulated phishing attack to test your organization’s end-user susceptibility to conduct attacker requested actions. The campaign can be conducted for all users in your organization or a selected control group.

Benefits for your Business

  • Assess if there is a need for a security awareness program/training inside your company
  • Better protect your online assets from becoming bait in a phishing attack
  • Decrease the chance of being a victim of a fraudulent action and help your workforce become skilled in spotting phishing attacks, by encouraging user vigilance
